I redesigned the Tekiro website to better reflect the brand’s identity of precision, reliability, and high-performance tools. The goal was to create a more modern and user-friendly experience, making it easier for users to explore product information ranging from hand tools to cordless equipment for both light repairs and heavy-duty tasks. This redesign also helps present Tekiro’s quality and durability more clearly for both professionals and everyday users.
